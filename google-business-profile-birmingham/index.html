Google Business Profile FAQ

I already have a Google Business Profile — can you fix it?

Yes. Most profiles we audit have wrong categories, thin descriptions, or missing photos. We'll optimise what you have or rebuild it properly — included in your package price.

How do reviews affect my ranking?

Reviews are a major local ranking factor. Quantity, recency, and keywords in reviews all matter. We set up a review request system so you can build reviews consistently without awkward asks.

Do I own my Google Business Profile?

Yes — always. We set it up under your Google account and hand you full access. No agency lock-in, ever.
