Why your Google Business Profile matters more than your website

For most local searches in Birmingham, Google shows the Map Pack above all other results. Three businesses. That's it. If you're not in that pack, most searchers never see you — no matter how good your website is.

A fully optimised Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is the single highest-ROI action most Birmingham small businesses can take. And unlike ads, the calls it generates don't cost you per click.

Google Business Profile FAQ

I already have a Google Business Profile — can you fix it?

Yes. Most profiles we audit have wrong categories, thin descriptions, or missing photos. We'll optimise what you have or rebuild it properly — included in your package price.

How do reviews affect my ranking?

Reviews are a major local ranking factor. Quantity, recency, and keywords in reviews all matter. We set up a review request system so you can build reviews consistently without awkward asks.

Do I own my Google Business Profile?

Yes — always. We set it up under your Google account and hand you full access. No agency lock-in, ever.

How quickly will I see more calls?

GBP improvements can show results faster than traditional SEO — often within 30–60 days for review velocity and profile completeness. Map Pack position typically follows within 60–90 days.
