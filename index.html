Why a one-off price and not a monthly plan?

Because you shouldn't have to pay forever to own something that's yours. We build it, you own it — website, GBP access, all content, everything. If you want ongoing support after that, it's your choice, not a condition.

How long until I see results?

Your website goes live in 10–14 days. GBP and SEO improvements typically show within 60–90 days, depending on how competitive your area is. We set honest expectations from day one.

I already have a website — can you work with it?

We'll review it in the free audit. If it's solid, we'll optimise it. If it's holding you back, we'll rebuild it. Either way, it's included in your package price — no extra charge.

What's included in the monthly growth plans?

GBP management covers posts, photo uploads, Q&A, and review chasing. SEO & content covers monthly keyword tracking, ranking reports, and one blog post. The £199 bundle includes both — and you can cancel with 30 days notice, any time.

Do you work with businesses outside Birmingham?

Our focus is the West Midlands — Birmingham, Solihull, Wolverhampton, Coventry, Walsall. Local focus is exactly what makes local SEO work. We're not trying to be everywhere.

What's the free audit and what does it cost?

Nothing. We review your current Google rankings, GBP setup, website speed, and your top 3 local competitors — then email you a plain-English report. No call required, no sales pitch attached.
