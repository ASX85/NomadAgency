What local SEO actually does for your business

Local SEO is how Google decides which businesses to show when someone nearby searches for a service. It covers your website, your citations, your reviews, and how well your Google Business Profile aligns with what people are searching for.

For Birmingham trades, clinics, restaurants, and professional services, ranking in the top 3 of the Map Pack often means the difference between a quiet week and a fully booked one.

Areas we serve

We focus on the West Midlands — Birmingham, Solihull, Wolverhampton, Coventry, and Walsall. Local focus is exactly what makes local SEO work. We don't try to be everywhere.

GBP is one part of local SEO — it's your listing on Google Maps. Local SEO also covers your website, citations, reviews, and the broader signals Google uses to rank you. We handle both.
